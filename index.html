What is the estimated average glucose calculator?
The estimated average glucose calculator is a tool that converts the value of glycated hemoglobin to the average blood sugar level in the past 3 months.
How to calculate estimated average glucose?
To calculate estimated average glucose you should use the following formula:
estimated average glucose = (28.7 * HbA1c) - 46.7
